Our story

More than just
a drink

Dora was born from a deep love for Japanese tea culture and the desire to share that quiet, mindful moment with Belgrade. Pure. Fresh. Matcha. — not just a tagline, it's how we do everything.

We serve only ceremonial grade matcha, carefully selected from the finest tea fields in Japan, prepared with precision and care. No rush. No noise. Just you and your cup.
